Welcome to our Home!

Vitalis was founded in 1997 and is located in the historic Gut Wehlitz in Schkeuditz near Leipzig. Our work and passion is to work with European trainees, students - for professional development and exchange of experiences through European exchange programmes like Erasmus+. Over the past 26 years, our Wehlitz estate has become an international place where groups from all over Europe meet to learn with and from each other. We now receive more than 1500 participants every year, taking part in different types of mobility projects.

The Headquarters of Vitalis found here houses and teaches the students. Rules are in play to maintain discipline, order, and safe and secure environment for work and study. We offer a place to meet new people and where students, through soft skills, like communication and teamwork, learn to communicate, meet, and enjoy their time together. Our mentors ensure disciplined work while simultaneously offering their advices and companionship to their students, passing their skills and experience to them. We offer not only accommodation, but also meals and snacks throughout the day. We don't just work, but travel together too!
